A Bit About Me

Hey there! I'm Carlos Alberto Bustamante Gaytán. I have a solid background in engineering and a big passion for data science. With a Master's degree in Engineering Sciences from Tecnológico de Monterrey, I dive into machine learning, data analytics, and advanced statistical modeling.

My journey started in mechatronics, where I learned how to combine mechanical systems with smart algorithms. Now, I use this foundation to create data-driven solutions that solve complex problems. Whether I'm building predictive models or turning raw data into useful insights, I always bring a detail-oriented and results-focused approach to my work.

Outside of the technical stuff, I'm all about continuous learning and working together with others. I truly believe that data science has the power to drive informed decisions and spark innovation. Let's connect, explore, and create something amazing together!
