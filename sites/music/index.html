The Producer

Carlos Bustamante, better known as Charlyfive, is an electronic music producer who discovered his passion for production at the age of 15. Inspired by the legendary sounds of Hardwell, Dannic, and Swedish House Mafia, he quickly developed his own signature style in the Future Bounce and Big Room genres.

With releases on labels like Zibiria Records and Mystic Network, Charlyfive has earned support from industry heavyweights including DANNIC, TWIIG, D3FAI, ANG, and more. His productions blend powerful drops with melodic elements, creating tracks that resonate on dancefloors worldwide.
